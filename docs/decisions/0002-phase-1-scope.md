# Decision 0002: Phase 1 Scope

## Status

Proposed

## Decision

Phase 1 will build one polished vertical slice instead of attempting the full RealLabVerse vision at once.

## Included

- Student authentication
- Hostel room
- Campus path
- Department building entry
- Laboratory access gate
- One realistic lab
- PPE and attendance workflow
- One experiment
- Basic NPC behavior
- Basic audio
- Mistake tracking
- Contamination tracking
- Faculty dashboard MVP

## Not Included Yet

- Full campus
- Multiple labs
- Multiplayer
- Voice chat
- Advanced AI NPCs
- Institute SaaS customization
- All experiments

## Reason

The product vision is large. A focused vertical slice allows the team to prove the simulation, training, and reporting loop before expanding.
